Exhibition guidelines for applicants.

Point of this JAPAN Drugstore Show exhibition

Promoting "Ganbaru Japan" and "Self-Medication Declaration", the drugstore industry is united and stimulates demand centering on the Japan Drugstore Show.This time as well, the show is scheduled to attract more exhibitors, and many projects will be implemented to further enhance the superiority of exhibitors.

Point1 - Held at Tokyo Big Sight, the largest and leading exhibition venue in Japan!

Due to its convenient location near the city center and airport, we can expect many visitors not only from the Kanto area, but also from all over Japan and around the world.

Point2 - Business negotiation targets seeking business opportunities with your company will definitely come!

The exhibition is organized by the Japan Chain Drugstore Association which is an industry group organized by domestic drugstore chains, so it’s sure to provide business matching opportunities with drugstores for you. In addition, exhibiting at the "For business" venue dedicated to business negotiations will help to increase the attention of business negotiation targets and provide an opportunity to expand sales channels.

Point3 - Extensive exhibition environment & various menus to maximize benefits
  • Increase appeal with product-specific zoning (12 zones)
  • Strengthen attention zones to create new markets (fem care, SDGs, food and health)
  • The trial (mini) booth, no worry even for first-time exhibitors!
  • Various support menus to improve the effects of exhibiting
  • Free listing on homepages and guidebooks
  • Free provision of visitor mobilization tools (invitations, posters, etc.)
  • Business matching with major drugstore chains and distributors
  • Full menu for exhibitor to promote business negotiations and PR

＜Special corner for Newly released and featured category products, etc.＞

Point4 - Simultaneous approach to both B (business) and C (consumer)

In addition to business people, this show attracts a large number of beauty and health-conscious consumers and consumers, and the exhibitors can promote their companies and products directly. There are many visitors not only from the major drugstore chains but also trading companies, wholesalers, convenience stores, supermarkets, and other retailers, and we support active business negotiations, transactions, and network building.

Point5 – Diverse Promotion Strategies to attract visitors
  • Posting notices in leading media such as trade journals, economic newspapers, and digital advertisements
  • Disseminate event information using the official website, official SNS and other SNS ( exhibitors, drugstores, etc.)!
  • A large number of managers and buyers from major drugstore chains will surely visit
  • Total number of invitations distributed will be about 1.5 million
  • Actively attracting visitors from exhibitors' customer channels by utilizing business invitations (Japanese, English, Chinese, Korean)
  • Actively mobilize by distributing invitations and posters displayed at drugstores nationwide
  • Attract coverage from many influential media during the exhibition period
Point6 - Enhanced official website functions

The online exhibition function, which has been well received by both business and general visitors in the past two shows, is added to the existing official website! As well as the real exhibition, you can get information from anywhere at any time through archive distribution of exhibitor information and seminars online. We have received a lot of favorable comments from people who are too far away to visit the event, and people who like to enjoy it again after coming to the event!

  • Various event information
  • Event stage program
  • Seminar program
  • Exhibitor and product information
  • Stage/seminar archive distribution
  • Pre-visit registration (business only)

  • Regarding the form of the standard booth

    There are three types of booths: single row, double row, and independent booths. (L-shaped placement is not possible)

    If you like to have an independent booth (10 booths or more in principle), you will be given only booth space.
    Only exhibitors who apply for 12 or more booths will be able to transform their independent booths.

  • If you apply for 10 or more standard booths, we will provide free booths.

    ① Apply for 10 booths : one booth free
    (Example) 10 booths → Fee for 9 booths (1 booth free)

    ② Apply for 10 booths or more: one booth free for every 6 booths
    (Example) 16 booths → Fee for 14 booths (2 booths free)
    22 booths → Fee for 19 booths (3 booths free)
